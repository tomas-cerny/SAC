REGISTRATION

Please read before you start your online registration. The Online Registration link is provided at the bottom of this page.

REGISTRATION BENEFITS

  • Proceedings:

    SAC 2018 Proceedings will be provided in electronic format on a USB. Every registered attendee receives one USB.

  • Registration Benefits:

    Every registered attendee has access to Tuesday reception; Tuesday keynote speaker session; and all technical sessions, lunches, and coffee breaks on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday. The registration includes one USB proceedings, one complimentary bag, and one banquet ticket (Thursday).

    An author who has accepted paper and poster must register as Paper Author to cover both publications. Additional papers and posters require additional registrations for inclusion in the conference proceedings.

    Graduate students participating in the Student Research Competition (SRC) program are required to register at the student rate.

    Please note that the discounted Student Registration rate does NOT cover paper or poster inclusion in the conference proceedings. Student registration is intended to encourage student attendance and participation. Student registration includes one banquet ticket.

  • Page Limit and Extra Page Fee:

    Full papers are limited to 6 pages, in camera-ready format, included in the registration fee. Authors have the option to include up to two (2) extra pages at additional fee of US$80 per page.

    Posters are limited to 3 pages, in camera-ready format, included in the registration fee. Authors have the option to include only one (1) extra page at additional fee of US$80.

    SRC Abstracts are limited to 2 pages, in camera-ready format, included in the registration fee. No extra pages allowed.
