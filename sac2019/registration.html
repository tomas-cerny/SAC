The registration page will open on Dec 1st 2018!

Please read before you start your online registration. The Online Registration link is provided at the bottom of this page.

  • Proceedings:

    SAC Proceedings will be provided in electronic format on a USB. Every registered attendee receives one USB.

  • Registration Benefits:

    Every registered attendee, including students, has access to Tuesday reception; keynote and technical sessions, lunches(excluding tutorial luncheon), coffee breaks, and Thursday banquet.
    Each registration includes one USB proceedings, one complimentary bag, and one banquet ticket.

    An author who has accepted paper and poster must register as Paper Author to cover both publications. Additional papers and posters require additional registrations for inclusion in the conference proceedings.

    Graduate students participating in the Student Research Competition (SRC) program are required to register at the student rate.

    Please note that the discounted Student Registration rate does NOT cover paper or poster inclusion in the conference proceedings. Student registration is intended to encourage student attendance and participation.

  • Page Limit and Extra Page Fee:

    Full papers are limited to 8 pages, in camera-ready format, included in the registration fee. Authors have the option to include up to two (2) extra pages at additional fee of € 70 per page.

    Posters are limited to 3 pages, in camera-ready format, included in the registration fee. Authors have the option to include only one (1) extra page at additional fee of € 70.

    SRC Abstracts are limited to 4 pages, in camera-ready format using the the SRC template, included in the registration fee. No extra pages allowed.

ONLINE REGISTRATION

  • Registrations will be done online through www.easyconferences.org. Participants may pay instantly using their credit card (VISA/MASTERCARD), or by bank transfer. A one-stop-shop software is used for the whole registration process, www.easyconferences.org. The whole process is very easy to use, it is totally secure and can be completed in a few minutes. Please note that participants may not only register for the conference through www.easyconferences.org, but they can also book their accommodation, airport transfers, car hire, etc. All additional services are offered at specially conference negotiated rates. Furthermore, the software provides participants with the possibility to complete the registration process periodically; participants may register for the conference and return later to book further services (subject to availability).

    Once you complete your online registration, a Confirmation Email is sent to you. Please save it for you.
    If you need help with your online registration, please email the Conference Registration Support sac19@easyconferences.org.
