REGISTRATION

The registration page will open around October 2018!

Please read before you start your online registration. The Online Registration link is provided at the bottom of this page.

  • Proceedings:

    SAC 2018 Proceedings will be provided in electronic format on a USB. Every registered attendee receives one USB.

  • Registration Benefits:

    Every registered attendee, including students, has access to Tuesday reception; keynote and technical sessions, lunches, coffee breaks, and Thursday banquet.
    Each registration includes one USB proceedings, one complimentary bag, and one banquet ticket.

    An author who has accepted paper and poster must register as Paper Author to cover both publications. Additional papers and posters require additional registrations for inclusion in the conference proceedings.

    Graduate students participating in the Student Research Competition (SRC) program are required to register at the student rate.

    Please note that the discounted Student Registration rate does NOT cover paper or poster inclusion in the conference proceedings. Student registration is intended to encourage student attendance and participation.

  • Payment Methods:

    1. Credit Card: AMEX, MC, and Visa are accepted.
    2. Bank Transfer: ACM bank account information is posted on the registration site. Please contact the registrar (hhaddad@kennesaw.edu) for questions.

    Author Registration (credit card or Bank Transfer) must be completed by Friday December 15, 2017 in order to ensure paper/poster inclusion in the conference proceedings.

    Registrations using the Bank Transfer option are confirmed once ACM confirmed the receipt of the payment.

  • Page Limit and Extra Page Fee:

    Full papers are limited to 8 pages, in camera-ready format, included in the registration fee. Authors have the option to include up to two (2) extra pages at additional fee of US$80 per page.

    Posters are limited to 3 pages, in camera-ready format, included in the registration fee. Authors have the option to include only one (1) extra page at additional fee of US$80.

    SRC Abstracts are limited to 2 pages, in camera-ready format using the the SRC template, included in the registration fee. No extra pages allowed.
